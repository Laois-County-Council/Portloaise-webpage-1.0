Laptop Guide

These HP ProBook G7 laptops have been prepared for families in Mountmellick.

Login Details

Passwordlaptopuser
1. Turning On the Laptop
  • 1
    Open the laptop screen.
  • 2
    Press the power button (usually above the keyboard).
  • 3
    Wait for the screen to turn on.
2. Logging In
  • 1
    Type the username: Admin
  • 2
    Type the password: laptopuser
  • 3
    Press Enter
3. Connecting to WiFi
  • 1
    Click the WiFi icon (bottom right corner).
  • 2
    Select your home network.
  • 3
    Enter your WiFi password.
  • 4
    Click Connect.
4. Using the Laptop
  • 1
    Use the touchpad or mouse to move the cursor.
  • 2
    Click to open apps.
  • 3
    Open a browser (like Chrome or Edge) to use the internet.
5. Charging the Laptop
  • 1
    Plug in the charger to the laptop.
  • 2
    Connect it to a wall socket.
  • 3
    You will see a battery icon charging.
6. Important Information
  • 1
    These are not company laptops.
  • 2
    No IT support is provided.
  • 3
    Keep your laptop safe and clean.
7. Need Help?
Please contact local community support services if you need assistance.
